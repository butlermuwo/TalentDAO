TALENT DAO,
GLOBAL SUMMIT
2022

The global summit brings together the community of experts, academic, and activists who comprise the Talent DAO affiliate network in a different crypto city every two years. This year we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of Talent DAO, web3 decentralized culture and open knowledge. So if you’re active and engaged in the worlds of Blockchain and talent building - hackers, open knowledge. Governments and foundations, lawyers and activists etc we hope you’ll consider joining us this year to build a stronger and more vibrant community together.

Please contact us per email for any further questions about Talent DAO Global summit 2022.

talentdao2022@talentdao.eth

Talent DAO global summit 2022 logo

The logo of Talent DAO global summit 2022 was decided through the logo competition from November 30th 2021 to December 15th 2021. The decision to select the winner was done by the Talent DAO community level on a quorum basis via quadratic voting. The logo from Butler Shimaluwani won the prize.
